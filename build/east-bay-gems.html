East Bay Gems

Summary

East Bay Gems was desgined to help users find and share interesting lesser-known people and places in the East Bay. Users can sign in through Google, once signed in, they can post and like and comment on gems in their area. The landing page shows a selection of featured gems. The main page is randomly populated with gems from all over the East Bay. On the right, you can filter based on gem-type, location, or type of venue. ​

This project was one of the first assignments that I completed for the General Assembly Web Developement Immersive. This was the first full stack application that I built using Node.js.
