Summary

I build two propietary trading platforms from the ground up. Both platforms make it easy to automate trading and configure notifications on Slack.

38 North is a cryptocurrency arbitrage trading application. It allows for easy configuration of bot trading between multiple cryptocurrency exchanges and across different fiat currencies.

Oracle Insights allows for configuration of negative risk orders and alerts on Predictit.
